Board Members

Manraj SinghPresident

He's participated in several competitions such as; CyberPatriot, CCDC, ISTS, ICL, NCL multiple times, and is planning on competing in several more competitions in the future. In his spare time, he loves toying with his cybersecurity home lab, running multiple VMs, including Windows Server, a home media server, a private tunneling solution, and many others.

Jack RosenbergVice President

Jack is a junior from Ypsilanti. He has been involved with the IASA for about a year and a half now, and has gone to ISTS, CCDC, NCL, NCAE, and more in his time with the club. He has placed 16 out of over 7000 in NCL in Spring 2024. Recently, he has been experimenting with blue team automation through Ansible. His areas of expertise include reverse engineering and firewall administration, with much of this experience stemming from competitions. In his free time, he enjoys cooking, video games, biking, and tinkering with hardware.

Jarrett SignorettiWebmaster

Jarrett Signoretti is a senior student from Canton, Michigan. He's participated in several competitions. At the 2020 Michigan Governor's High School Cyber Challenge, his team won 3rd place. He placed 151st out of around three thousand competitors in the Fall 2021 National Cyber Scholar Competition. In the Fall 2023 season, he finished 27th out of nearly eight thousand students in the individual NCL game. Working together with other IASA members, they received the diamond medal by placing 13th out of 4,672 teams. His hobbies include coding in Python, creating instrumental music with FL Studio, and learning cybersecurity outside the classroom. So far, a few of his favorite topics in the field are web security, the Internet of Things, and botnets.
