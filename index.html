About IASA

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) guides and aids students interested in or pursuing a career in the Information Assurance field. All majors are welcome! This Winter 2025 semester, come hang out at the IASA meetings Wednesdays at 6:00 p.m. in Sill 157 or online! (Unless informed otherwise via Discord or Instagram.)

Our objective is to aid students in the pursuit of a career in information assurance or cyber security. We also further awareness about Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance & Cyber Defense (IACD) program, and help guide students interested in pursuing said major. We provide information and opportunities for students to develop and further their skills. Meetings will consist of student-led and professional presentations, hands on practice, and student interactions.

Sill Hall, 109 Jones-Goddard, Ypsilanti, MI 48197
