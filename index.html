About IASA

Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance Student Association (IASA) guides and aids students interested in or pursuing a career in the Information Assurance field. All majors are welcome! Come hangout at the IASA meetings every Thursday at 2:00pm! (Unless informed otherwise via Discord or Instagram.)

Our objective is to aids students in the pursuit of a career in information assurance or cyber security. We also furthering awareness about Eastern Michigan University's Information Assurance & Cyber Defense (IACD) program, and help guide students interested in pursuing said major. We providing information and opportunities for students to develop and further their skills. Meetings will consist of student-led and professional presentations, hands on practice, and student interactions.

The meetings are held online and in Room 157 Sill Hall, 109 Jones-Goddard, Ypsilanti, MI 48197.
